Kitchen Closing SOP:
1. Turn off gas stove
2. Clean all utensils
3. Store food in fridge
4. Sanitize all surfaces

Table Cleaning SOP:
1. Remove used plates
2. Wipe table with cloth
3. Spray sanitizer
4. Arrange chairs properly

Safety Rules:
1. Wear gloves in kitchen
2. Keep floor dry
3. Report accidents immediately

Cash Counter SOP:
1. Count total cash
2. Match billing records
3. Lock cash drawer
4. Submit daily report

Customer Service SOP:
1. Greet customers politely
2. Take orders clearly
3. Handle complaints calmly
4. Thank customer after service
